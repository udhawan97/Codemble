# Codemble workflow notes

- The public website is the Astro/Starlight site in `docs-site/`, deployed at
  `https://udhawan97.github.io/Codemble/` from GitHub Pages.
- “Apple-level”, “world class”, and “go all out” mean ambitious composition and
  interaction craft, not a request to replace Codemble’s Formal Edo identity.
- Codemble’s public visual vocabulary is an Edo star atlas: kachi indigo,
  gofun paper, ruri for interaction, and kohaku only for understanding.
- Prefer real product captures from `docs-site/public/shots/` over stock,
  generated, or invented interface media.
- The primary landing-page action is **Chart your project**. Documentation
  pages prioritize long-form reading and should not inherit cinematic motion.
- Site work is not complete at source/build success: verify compact widths,
  dark/light, reduced motion, keyboard behavior, CI, Pages, and the live URL.
- When explicitly authorized for this workflow, finish the branch path by
  merging and pushing `main`; do not stop after a local commit.
